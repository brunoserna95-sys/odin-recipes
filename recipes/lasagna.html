Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pasta
  • Meal
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Make a delicius tomato sauce
  2. Do the pasta in a caserola with boiling water
  3. Chop the onion
  4. Chop the meal
  5. Once the pasta is ready, make a layer of pasta in a bakery pan
  6. Put a little bit of tomato sauce and distribute in all the pasta surface
  7. Portion the meal over the tomato and the pasta
  8. Cover the layer with the pasta and repeat
